
Sweetgreen, Inc. (NYSE:SG) Q1 2024 Earnings Conference Call Summary

Key Points for Investors:
- Revenue: $157.9 million, 26% year-over-year growth.
- Same-store sales: 5%.
- Digital sales: 59% of total revenue.
- Restaurant-level margin: 18.1%, expanded over 400 basis points year-over-year.
- Restaurant-level profit: $28.5 million, nearly 70% increase from a year ago.
- Positive adjusted EBITDA for the quarter.
- Six new restaurants opened in Q1 2024.
- Infinite Kitchens tracking to $2.6 million average unit volume with 28% margin.
- Seven new Infinite Kitchen restaurants expected to open in 2024, with more retrofits planned.
- New restaurant growth rate to return to 15%-20% per year starting in 2025.
- Turnover decreased by 19 points compared to Q1 2023.
- Cash balance: $244 million.
- Fiscal year 2024 guidance updated: 23-27 new restaurant openings, revenue $660-$675 million, same-store sales growth 4%-6%, restaurant level margin 18.5%-20%, adjusted EBITDA $10-$19 million.
